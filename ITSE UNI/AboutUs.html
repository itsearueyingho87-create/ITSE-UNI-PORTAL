Governance and University Senate

Vareya University operates within a well-defined governance framework that upholds accountability, transparency, academic freedom, and regulatory compliance in line with best practices for private universities.

Governing Council

The Governing Council is the highest decision-making body of the University. It provides strategic oversight and ensures the long-term sustainability of the institution.

  • Approval of institutional policies and strategic plans
  • Oversight of financial and physical resources
  • Appointment and supervision of principal officers
  • Ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements

University Executive Management

The Executive Management is responsible for the administration and daily operations of the University.

It is led by the Vice-Chancellor and includes the Deputy Vice-Chancellors, Registrar, Bursar, and other statutory officers.

  • Implementation of academic and administrative policies
  • Coordination of research, teaching, and learning
  • Human resource and institutional development
  • Student welfare and campus administration

University Senate

The Senate is the highest academic authority of the University. It is responsible for maintaining academic standards and regulating all academic matters.

  • Approval of academic programs and curricula
  • Regulation of examinations and assessments
  • Award of degrees, diplomas, and certificates
  • Supervision of teaching and research quality

Senate Composition

The Senate comprises the Vice-Chancellor as Chair, Deputy Vice-Chancellors, Deans of Faculties, Heads of Departments, Professors, and other senior academic staff as provided by the University statutes.

Through its committees, the Senate ensures that academic decisions are transparent, merit-based, and aligned with national and international standards.
