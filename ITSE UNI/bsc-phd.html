The Accelerated Advantage

Save Time

Bypass the traditional Master's degree. Complete your B.Sc and PhD in just 7 years of intensive, focused study.

Early Research Immersion

Begin working with world-class faculty on groundbreaking research projects starting in your sophomore year.

Full Fellowship

Accepted students receive a comprehensive fellowship covering tuition, research materials, and a living stipend.

The Pathway (7-Year Timeline)

Y1

Foundation & Core Sciences (Years 1-2)

Complete rigorous undergraduate coursework while being matched with a faculty research mentor.

Y3

Advanced Study & Thesis (Years 3-4)

Finish your B.Sc requirements. Defend an undergraduate thesis that serves as the foundation for your doctoral proposal.

Y5

Doctoral Research (Years 5-7)

Full-time doctoral research, publications, conference presentations, and final dissertation defense.
